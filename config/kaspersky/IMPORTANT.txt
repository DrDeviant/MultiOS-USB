Kaspersky Rescue Disk
url="https://rescuedisk.s.kaspersky-labs.com/updatable/2018/krd.iso"

Kaspersky requires a non-standard directory with an ISO image. 

There are two ways to boot Kaspersky Rescue Disk:

1) save "krd.iso" image to "/data" directory on your MultiOS-USB; or
2) unpack "krd.iso" image to "/os_files/krd" directory on your MultiOS-USB
